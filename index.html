History of Independence Day

1940 – Lahore Resolution

The Lahore Resolution laid the foundation for Pakistan’s creation.

1947 – Independence

Pakistan was officially formed on 14th August, 1947.

1956 – First Constitution

Pakistan adopted its first constitution on 23rd March, 1956.

1965 – Defence Day

Pakistan defended itself during the 1965 war with great courage.
